Strawberry Smoothie

Description!

Follow along to make a very good strawberry smoothie

Ingredients

  • Strawberry
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ice Cream(Optional)

Steps

  1. Add the strawberry and milk into a blender.
  2. Blend it till required amount.
  3. Add vanilla ice cream to add texture and taste.
  4. If not sweet add some more sugar.
