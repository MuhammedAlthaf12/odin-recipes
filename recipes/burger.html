Burger

Description

This recipe is a follow along with the help of which you will be able to make a tasty burger

Ingredients

  • Zinger Burger
  • Bun
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise(Optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the pan with oil.
  2. Fry the burger until it looks cooked.
  3. Put the burger in the bun.
  4. Finally add lettuce and Ketchup.
