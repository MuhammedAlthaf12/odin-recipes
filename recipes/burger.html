Burger

A brief about the recipes

This is an easy way to make a burger at home and it takes only a few minutes and is an easy breakfast to prepare in the morning occasionally

Ingredients

  • A frozen packet of burgers
  • Buns
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Mayonaise and any other condiments that you would like to add

Steps for making the burger

  1. Heat a pan and then heat the burger on it and cook it till it is a dark brown color
  2. Once the patty has been made, place it on the bun
  3. Add a 2 slices of tomatos and place a slice of lettuce on it
  4. And finally put any sauces and condiments that you would like to add on it
